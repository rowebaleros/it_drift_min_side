why connect two pc's via LAN?

WARNING: this only works for windows 10

One of the main advantages of connecting two pc's is that it provides a medium to share files when you do not have access to a wireless network.

step 1:

Go to "control panel" then click on "network and internet", after that click on "network and sharing center" and then click on "change adapter settings".

step 2:

After you have clicked on "change adapter settings" it will reveal different contexts. You need to choose the right connection for your LAN, that would usually be called "ethernet".

step 3:

Right-click on the connection and select properties, then the site connection porperties window should appear.

step 4:

Under the "network" tab, select ipv4, short for "internet protocol version 4" and then click "properties". Once you are in the properties menu, set the IP adress and subnet mask of the first computer to for example:

IP - 192.168.0.5

subnet mask - 225.225.225.0

Now repeat all the steps above for the second pc, setting the IP adress and subnet mask as follows:

IP - 192.168.0.5

subnet mask - 225.225.225.0

step 5:

After assigning the IP adresses, you will need to return to the network and sharing center and then click on "change advanced sharing settings".

step 6:

Now when you are in the advanced sharing settings menu, you have to enable the "turn on network discovery" and "turn on automatic setup network connected devices" options. You can also select the file sharing and printer sharing option. this will give you the second pc access to the files and printers shared on the network

step 7:

Right-click on "this pc" and select "properties". After that click "change settings" and then "change". That will reveal a window with the name of the task force. The workgroup name value should be the same for both pc's. By default, the workgroup name is WORKGROUP, but you can easily change it to what you like.

step 8:

Right-click on the drive you want to share, then scroll to the "grant access to" option and click "advanced sharing". Click the "advanced sharing" button under the sharing tab. This reveals the advanced sharing window. Check the "share this folder" box and click "apply" and then "OK". You have now successfully linked two pc's to share your drives between them.
