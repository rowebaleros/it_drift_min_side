PAUL "PG-13" GEORGE

WHO IS PAUL GEORGE, AND WHAT DOES HE DO FOR A LIVING?

Paul Clifton Anthony George was born May 2, 1990. He is an American professional basketball player for the Los Angeles Clippers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He is also known as "PG-13" or "PG-24", Paul George is an eight-time NBA All-Star and six-time member of the All-NBA Team, as well as a four-time member of the NBA All-Defensive Team.

NBA CAREER START

Paul George declared for the 2010 nba draft right out of Fresno State College, and at the 10th pick in the 1st round of the 2010 nba draft, he was drafted by the Indiana Pacers where he stayed until 2017 when he requested a trade to the Oklahoma City Thunder, to join forces with prime time Russell Westbrook, Carmelo Anthony and Steven Adams. he stayed in Oklahoma for 2 years, before making his way to one of the teams he rooted for since he was a child, the Los Angeles Clippers where he then joined forces with Kawhi Leonard, Bones Hyland and was reunited with his old teammate from the Oklahoma City Thunder, Russell Westbrook.
